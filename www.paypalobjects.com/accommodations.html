Partial or Full Subsidies Available

While the amount available for reimbursement varies from year to year depending on the number of teams registered and availability of funds, we anticipate that we will be able to provide at least a partial subsidy, if not a full subsidy, for those teams requiring accomodation. Further information will become available on March 6th, and subsidies will be provided after the event upon submission of receipts of expenditures. Please note that subsidies are available ONLY for accomodation, but bear in mind that your registration fee also provides for 2 day's worth of breakfast & lunch as well as our social event.
