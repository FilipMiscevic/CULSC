Registration is now open!

The deadline to register is Friday, March 6th, 2015 at 5:00pm. On Friday, March 6th, sign-in and last minute team registration will open at 5:00pm until 7:00pm - please arrive between these times. Sign-in will take place in Wetmore Hall, New College, at the University of Toronto (St. George campus). Here is a link to a map indicating its location http://map.utoronto.ca/building/032.
