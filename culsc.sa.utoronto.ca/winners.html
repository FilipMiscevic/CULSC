    Results from 2011

    The first annual CULSC has drawn to a close! Many excellent teams showed up this year from the University of Guelph, York University, and University of Toronto to put their skills and knowledge to the test as they were challenged in six different categories of the life sciences.

    Four of the highest scoring teams moved on to the finals, where DMAK took fourth place, the Perpetual Motion Squad claimed bronze, Quadruple Bypass claimed silver, and the Major Groovers took the gold.

    York University demonstrated excellent overall preparedness as they claimed the prize for 'Top Overall University', and Guelph University's Spermatogeniuses were awarded as the 'Most Spirited Team'.

    We want to again congratulate all of our winners and thank all the competitors for coming out this year! We hope to see you again next year!

    • 1st Place: Major Groovers
    • 2nd Place: Quadruple Bypass
    • 3rd Place: Perpetual Motion Squad (PMS)
    • 4th Place: DMAK
    • Top Overall University: York University
    • Most Spirited Team: Spermatogeniuses

    The medalists were determined in the Final Round.
