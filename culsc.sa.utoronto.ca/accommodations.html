Being Updated

We are currently working towards partially subsidizing or providing accommodations for our guests from out of town. This will be dependent on participation numbers as well as a few other factors, so at this point it cannot be guaranteed. We will keep you posted on this! The information from last year's event has been kept so that you may take note of their accommodations

Updates

    Global Village Kensington

    The first property is a student residence with mostly private rooms equipped with double beds, cable tv, free wireless, writing desk and balcony. The front desk is open 9:00 to 9:00 and students have 24 access to the property with their keys. There is a kitchen and common room also available.

    Website:www.globalvillagekensington.com

    Email:info@collegehostel.com

    Address:280 Augusta Avenue (just off College St.)

    Price per Night:$66 (private) or $24 (dorm bed, these are limited)

    Internet:Free Wireless

    Global Village Backpackers

    Full backpackers bar, 24-hr reception, swipe-card security, air conditioning, free wireless internet, huge common areas, outdoor patio and travel desk.

    Website:www.globalbackpackers.com/

    Email:info@globalbackpackers.com

    Address:460 King Street West (corner of King St. W and Spadina)

    Price per Night:$26 (6-10 bed dorms) with $10 linen/key deposit

    Internet:$24.50 CAD

    Canadiana Backpackers Inn

    All you can eat pancake breakfast, Morning coffee & tea all day, Bed Linens, Fun organized activities every day, tickets to local attractions, Secure luggage storage, Air Conditioning, 24 Hour Security, 18 Seat Movie Theatre, Air-conditioned rooms, Large bathrooms, Fully equipped kitchen, TV/DVD lounge, Outdoor patio

    Website:www.canadianalodging.com/

    Email:info@canadianalodging.com

    Address:42 Widmer St.

    Price per Night:From $29 (10 bed dorms) to $70 (single private)

    Internet:Free Wireless, Kiosks with half hour free
